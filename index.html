Climate Change

In this collection of readings, I am tracing the idea of the commons and who is the responsible party for climate change. Additionally, I enjoyed thinking about the internet as a form of the commons, especially given what is happening in social media.

Garrett Hardin - Tragedy of the Commons
J.B. Jackson - The Places We Play
Michelle Nijhuis - Miracles of the Commons
Vangelis Papadimitropoulos - Commons-Based Peer Production in the work of Yochai Benkler
John McPhee - Los Angeles Against the Mountains
